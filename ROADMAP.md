1. Project foundation — selesai pada scaffold awal.
2. UI shell dan state management — selesai pada scaffold awal.
3. PDF.js viewer — render halaman, thumbnail, zoom, navigasi, pencarian.

7. Conversion engine — LibreOffice, PDF-to-Office, table extraction.
8. Printing — preview dan pilihan printer.
9. Integration hardening — jobs, progress, cancel, logging, recovery.
10. First full run, testing, lalu packaging portable/installer.
